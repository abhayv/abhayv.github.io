Abhay Vardhan

I am an entreprenuer and hacker with deep experience in mobile apps, consumer web, analytics, search and big data. I am the co-founder of Blippy, a cool way to discover GIFs and share them with your friends.

The story of my work life starts in Urbana, Illinois where I joined Motorola and helped develop one of the very first implementations of Bluetooth on mobile phones. I was always passionate about programming and software, so I decided to pursue a PhD at the University of Illinois while working.

After completing my PhD, I decided to take up a new challenge and moved to California to join Google. Here, I worked in the core web search team where I got hands-on experience in building huge scale systems and worked on challenging problems in ranking, search evaluation and analysis of large amounts of data.

I could not resist startups for too long. I joined BloomReach, where, as the first hire, I helped build the core search based relevance engine. The BloomReach experience was an eye opener and directly inspired me to launch Blippy.
